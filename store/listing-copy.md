## Full Description
*(16,000 chars max — supports limited HTML: `<b>`, `<i>`, `<ul>`, `<li>`, `<br>`)*

```
<b>Stop writing proposals from scratch. Let AI do it for you.</b>

UpWrite reads the job posting you're looking at on Upwork, sends it to your AI backend, and automatically fills in your cover letter and screening questions — all without leaving the page.

<b>How it works</b>

1. Open any Upwork proposal page.
2. Click the UpWrite icon in your toolbar.
3. Optionally record a short intro video or attach a screenshot for extra context.
4. Click <b>Generate Proposal</b>.
5. Review the AI-generated text, edit anything you want, and hit submit.

That's it. No copy-pasting. No tab-switching. No blank-page anxiety.

<b>Key Features</b>

<b>• One-click proposal extraction</b>
UpWrite reads the job title, full description, client name, budget, cover-letter field, and every screening question directly from the page — including hidden text behind "read more" buttons.

<b>• Video context (optional)</b>
Record your screen, record a quick camera intro, upload a file, or paste any video URL. UpWrite attaches it to your AI request so the model can reference what you actually do.

<b>• Auto-fill that works with Upwork's form</b>
Most extensions break on React-controlled inputs. UpWrite fires the correct synthetic events so Upwork's form registers every keystroke — no more fields that look filled but submit blank.

<b>• Editable response before submitting</b>
Every AI-generated answer appears in an editable panel inside the popup. Tweak the tone, add specifics, or copy everything to the clipboard with one click.

<b>• Works even on backgrounded tabs</b>
If you switch away while your proposal is generating, UpWrite stores the result and fills the form the moment you come back to the tab.

<b>• Your AI, your data</b>
UpWrite connects to a webhook you configure — your own n8n flow, Make scenario, or any HTTPS endpoint. Your proposal data never touches our servers.

<b>Privacy</b>

UpWrite sends proposal data only to the webhook URL you explicitly configure. It does not collect, store, or transmit any data to UpWrite's developers. See our full privacy policy at: https://upwrite.app/privacy

<b>Requirements</b>

• Chrome 116 or later
• An Upwork account with access to the Proposals section
• An HTTPS webhook endpoint (e.g. n8n, Make, Zapier, or a custom API) that returns AI-generated proposal text
```

How It Works

From Job Listing to Winning Proposal
in 4 Simple Steps

🔍
1

Open an Upwork Proposal

Navigate to any job on Upwork and click "Submit a Proposal." UpWrite activates automatically on the proposal page.

📋
2

Data Is Extracted

The extension reads the job title, full description, client name, budget, cover-letter field, and any follow-up questions directly from the page.

🤖
3

AI Writes Your Proposal

All extracted data is sent to your private n8n webhook. Your AI workflow crafts a tailored cover letter and answers every client question.

✅
4

Form Is Auto-Filled

UpWrite receives the AI response and instantly populates the Upwork cover-letter field and all follow-up answers. Review and submit!

Installation

Get Up and Running in 5 Minutes

UpWrite is distributed as an unpacked Chrome extension — no Chrome Web Store needed.

Download the Extension

Click the button below to download the .zip file and unzip it to a folder on your computer.

Open Chrome Extensions

In Chrome, navigate to chrome://extensions and enable Developer Mode using the toggle in the top-right corner.

Load Unpacked

Click "Load unpacked" and select the unzipped upwrite/ folder. The UpWrite icon will appear in your toolbar.

Enter Your Webhook URL

Click the UpWrite toolbar icon, go to Settings, and paste your n8n webhook URL. Save — you're ready to go!
